About This Project

The Maryland Doctor Discipline Tracker collects and analyzes disciplinary actions taken by the Maryland Board of Physicians against health professionals. Records go back to 2014 and are updated regularly from the Board's public filings.

AI-generated summaries and keyword extraction help make these public documents more searchable and accessible.
